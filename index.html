Our Company



ARC Megawide started as a business consultancy on December 18, 2014, registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission as a Private Partnership Company of Ms. Norajane V. Torralba and Mr. Arvin T. Crisostomo. Its aim was to provide services to Individuals and Corporations with their acquisition, consolidation, and other real estate property needs. Such as case studies to maximize their property's income, cost analysis, and conceptual plans for several business models. Our in-house licensed real estate brokers do the validation and registration for landowners and their pertinent property documents. We tap finance experts to source for investments both from local and foreign financial institutions and other intermediaries.


Through the years, the company had reached into an agreement to venture with a landowner and upgrade the corporation’s primary and secondary purposes into real estate, land development, construction, buying raw land and selling after developing them, and designing a general plan and cause the construction of mix uses. Under the new AMMON VENTURES INC. while keeping the ARC Megawide Dev’t Company as its trade name. It has applied for an authorized capital of Php 500 million pesos, primarily to engage in a much bigger project.


The Company President and Chief Executive – Arvin Tumulak Crisostomo chose the project of Dulos Realty and Development Corporation as its pilot project under the former venture with D.M. Wenceslao and Associates. From its former project name “La Vista Del Mar” in Cavite City, We now rebrand the project as the “Sangley West Bay City” under the new venture and new team of experts on board. This project will comprise of commercial complex, mid to high residences, hotels, and casinos that will complement the future Sangley International Airport. This project is expected to spur the economy of Cavite City, augment its zoning and land use and enhance its revenue, as it will also generate jobs to a more vibrant community and business environment.


The list goes on with promising projects involving urban housing development and other mining quarry concessions that we will soon include as the company progresses from its long-mile projected journey.
